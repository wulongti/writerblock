Welcome to the writerBlock app! This app will help you keep track of how many words you've written on your various books. If you like, it can even enter your progress into our LeaderBoard so you can see how you're doing against your fellow authors.

This app uses cookies to keep track if you're logged in, and we store your progress in our database, so that we can sync your progress across devices. If you would prefer to keep all of your data and progress to yourself then use the Single Device option below, but be warned: if you clear your localStorage or your cookies, you will lose all of your progress and data, and your progress will only be accessible from THIS device and THIS browser.
